Access by Car

  • From Lyon and Valence, follow signs to Chambéry / Rocade Sud. Take exit 1 – Domaine Universitaire / Gières Mayencin.
  • From Chambéry, follow signs to Gières Domaine Universitaire. Take exit 1 – Gières Mayencin / Domaine Universitaire.
  • From Grenoble city centre, take avenue Gabriel Péri towards St Martin d'Hères, then enter the campus in front of Géant Casino.

Access by Tram (from Grenoble Train Station)

  • Take Tram Line B, direction Gières Plaine des Sports.
  • Get off at Gabriel Fauré stop.
  • The IMAG building is a 5-10 minute walk from the tram stop.
